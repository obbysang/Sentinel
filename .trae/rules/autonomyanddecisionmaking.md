# Autonomy and Decision-Making — Sentinel

## Overview

Sentinel is designed to operate **without human intervention**. Its core function is to monitor construction site video feeds, reason over observations, and autonomously log safety violations.  

This document details:

- The autonomous decision-making process  
- Trigger conditions and thresholds  
- Temporal reasoning rules  
- Fail-safe and reflection mechanisms  
- Evidence of true agentic behavior  

By explicitly defining **when and how the agent acts**, this document demonstrates Sentinel’s **autonomy** to judges and stakeholders.
